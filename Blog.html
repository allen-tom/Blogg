Was the decision of James Madison to got to war with Britian the right thing to do for America?

Posted on April 28th, 2014 by Allen Tom - 3 comments

The War of 1812 was a war that was not necessary for the evolution of America. Even though America was annoyed by the British ships bombarding and destroying American ships, if they had faced this problem with peaceful terms, they could have avoided the war. The impatient march into war was backfiring on the Americans at first. The overpowering British navy was breezing thorugh the American defenses and they even got to level of invading that led to the inevitable burning of Washington D.C. and the White House. America was lucky to win the war because British had other wars with other European nations at the same time. The Battle of New Orleans led Andrew Jackson to Presidency.
